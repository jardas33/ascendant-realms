# v0.95 Capture Site And Label Density Report

## Capture-Site Presentation

Capture sites remain functionally unchanged. v0.95 adjusts visible state priority:

- Selected: brightest ring, selected label, full label visibility.
- Contested: amber warning ring, contested label, progress ring visible.
- Objective: Lume/teal objective emphasis.
- Friendly: quieter green ownership ring.
- Enemy: readable hostile ring without overpowering terrain.
- Neutral: resource-colored low-intensity ring.

## Label Density

The battle scene previously showed too many small labels during ordinary movement and combat. v0.95 keeps labels available but prioritizes labels that answer a player question:

- What did I select?
- What is my objective?
- Who is the commander or elite threat?
- Which site is contested or owned?
- Which building/production structure is this?

Routine unit labels are hidden until selected or statused, reducing text stacks over armies.

## Risks

- Test screenshots must confirm that hiding routine labels does not make selection feel empty.
- Final art will eventually replace many placeholder-label duties.
