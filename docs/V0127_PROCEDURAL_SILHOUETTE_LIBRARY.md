# v0.127 Procedural Silhouette Library

v0.127 adds a bounded procedural silhouette language for the existing Godot player-facing Salto review slice. It remains placeholder-only and uses script-owned Godot primitives.

## Unit Roles

- Aster hero: tall capsule profile with command banner, blade, shield plate, and crown disc.
- Worker: short compact body with crate pack and tool profile.
- Militia: six-sided melee body with shield and spear profile.
- Ranger: narrow ranged body with bow, quiver, and arrow line.
- Ashen Raider: tapered enemy body with horns and forward blade.
- Ashen Brute: optional larger enemy placeholder with broad shoulders and cleaver.

## Building And Site Roles

- Command Hall: keep tower, roof ridge, banner, and door slot.
- Barracks: training wings, weapon rack, and drill-yard edge.
- Mine/quarry: crane, mine-mouth shadow, cut-stone stack, and quarry terrain relationship.
- Shrine: round cap, beacon slot, and steps.
- Ruin: broken wall pocket and blocked edge.
- Capture site: claim disc, post, and contest tick.
- Lume endpoint: glow core, readability ring, and future art anchor.

No imported art, generated image, downloaded asset, final unit art, final building art, stable-ID change, save change, browser runtime change, final Godot choice, full port, or v0.128 work is included.
