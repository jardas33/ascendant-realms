## Retest Route

1. Start recording video.
2. Launch the game package.
3. Spend five seconds on the campaign map. Say aloud whether the map is clear enough immediately.
4. Click through the campaign tabs: Map, Stronghold, Hero, Inventory, Intel, Reputation.
5. Return to Map.
6. Use the private Lume demo launch button.
7. Capture West Stone Cut.
8. Capture Ford Toll.
9. Toggle Links Auto, Hidden, and Always.
10. Finish the demo.
11. Inspect compact Results.
12. Return to campaign.
13. Stop recording.

## Subjective Questions

Answer only these:

- Is the campaign map clear enough?
- Is the battle HUD readable enough?
- Are command buttons compact enough?
- Is the contextual Lume line helpful rather than annoying?
- Is the minimap readable enough?
- Is fog less ugly or still distracting?
- Is the Results screen concise enough?
- What remains confusing, ugly, or annoying?
