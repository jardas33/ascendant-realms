# V074 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

Use this checklist against the clean v0.72-v0.74 package.

## Battlefield Events

- Launch an eligible campaign battle with resource sites.
- Confirm the HUD can show a battlefield event title, objective, short timer/progress, and counterplay hint.
- Confirm events do not appear in Tutorial / Proving Grounds.
- Confirm events do not spam; only one major event should be active at a time.

## Dynamic Objectives

- For Site Under Threat, capture or defend the highlighted site and confirm the event resolves clearly.
- For Elite Strike, fight the elite squad and confirm the event label and elite readability are clear.
- Confirm failed event objectives read as failed or missed, not as persistent campaign failure.
- Confirm any event bonus feels battle-local and modest.

## Tactical Plan And Counterplay

- Select Guarded Advance, Resource Push, and Champion Hunt on eligible campaign battles.
- Confirm matching plan support appears in event HUD/Results when the plan helps the objective.
- Confirm the plan helps modestly but does not automatically complete the event.
- Confirm enemy doctrine/counterplay copy remains short and readable.

## Regression Checks

- Retinue deployment, recovery, reserves, and Call Retinue still work.
- Control groups, Patrol, formation-aware movement, Worker build/repair/site assignment, hero skills/relics, relic choice, replay rewards, and Act 1 unlock flow remain stable.
- Tutorial remains no-save/no-reward and does not show event complexity.
