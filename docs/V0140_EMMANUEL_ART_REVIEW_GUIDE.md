## Questions

- Which candidate best captures original Barrosan highland fantasy?
- Which candidate best supports a modern fixed-camera 2.5D RTS/RPG presentation?
- Can the road, ford, West Stone Cut Mine, shrine, ruin, Barracks area, Lume, and Ashen approach be named at a glance?
- Does any candidate feel too close to a protected game's visual identity?
- Does any candidate feel too much like mobile-game key art or too much like a dashboard/blockout?
- Which direction should be rejected, iterated, or used as a style reference for future prompts?

## Candidate Notes

- A: strongest tactical-clarity intent, with readable road, ford, quarry, shrine, ruin, build area, and Ashen edge.
- B: strongest atmosphere intent, with more mood, hearth warmth, moss, quarry detail, and a darker Ashen region.
- C: strongest balanced game-readiness intent, with clearer lane structure, UI-safe composition, Lume link, and practical build area.
