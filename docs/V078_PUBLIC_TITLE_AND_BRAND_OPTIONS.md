# v0.78 Public Title And Brand Options

Date: 2026-05-30

## Status

These are human-review proposals, not approved final commercial titles. Formal trademark, storefront, domain, localization, and legal review remain required before any public announcement.

The internal repository codename remains `Ascendant Realms` during v0.78. No runtime rebrand is authorized.

## Leading Recommendation

`JARDAS: Oath of the Barrosan Marches`

Recommendation: strongest current direction. It gives the game a short, ownable lead word and a subtitle that anchors the setting, oath theme, and highland frontier.

## Proposed In-World Meaning Of Jardas

A Jardas is an oath-bound hero awakened by the old pact with the land. A Jardas can awaken and bind Lume sites, but the role is not ownership. The Jardas defends, guides, and answers to the living power beneath the Marches.

The protagonist is a newly awakened Jardas from Salto. Their authority should feel earned through responsibility, not conquest.

## Brand Direction

- Logo hierarchy: `JARDAS` dominant, subtitle smaller.
- Brand mood: dark heroic-fantasy, highland oath, communal memory, harsh frontier.
- Avoid: generic fantasy kingdom names, copied RTS faction language, browser-game placeholder branding, mobile-style title treatment.

## Legal Note

No title in this document is legally cleared. Before commercial use, a qualified human legal/trademark review must evaluate trademark conflicts, domain/storefront viability, localization issues, and similarity risks.
