# v0.77 Emmanuel Retest Checklist - Act 1 Finale

Date: 2026-05-30

## Scope

Retest the Ashen Outpost Act 1 finale using existing campaign systems only. No new art, maps, or save migration are included.

## Route

1. Start or load an eligible campaign with Ashen Outpost unlocked.
2. Open Ashen Outpost and confirm briefing says Act 1 finale, Fortified Enemy, Captain Malrec, and tactical-plan guidance.
3. Launch with Champion Hunt.
4. Confirm the battle HUD shows Phase 1: Secure the Foothold.
5. Capture the Burned Shrine or another resource site.
6. Confirm phase copy advances toward breaking the fortified line.
7. Destroy the enemy Barracks.
8. Confirm Captain Malrec commits to the final defense.
9. Defeat Captain Malrec and destroy the Stronghold.
10. Confirm Results shows Act 1 Finale, commander defeated, phase completion, relic reward choice, skill reminder, Retinue summary when applicable, and replay-safe next steps.
11. Replay Ashen Outpost and confirm first-clear, rival, relic, and optional objective rewards do not duplicate.

## Pass Criteria

- Tutorial / Proving Grounds remains no-save/no-reward and has no finale phase noise.
- Malrec does not join early coordinated waves before the final phase.
- One battlefield event is active at a time.
- Results copy stays readable and does not hide relic, skill, Retinue, or replay information.
- Existing Worker, Patrol, control group, Retinue reinforcement, hero skill, and relic flows still behave normally.
