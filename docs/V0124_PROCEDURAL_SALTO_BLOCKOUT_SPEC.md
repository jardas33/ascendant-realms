# v0.124 Procedural Salto Blockout Spec

The player-facing 2.5D slice uses `CLEAN_READABILITY` as the default and fills the desktop viewport with a fixed high-angle orthographic camera.

## Procedural Blockout

- Moss and grass terrain posture.
- Wet-granite path strip and crossing readability strip.
- Shallow ford and water strip.
- Quarry cut and shadow.
- Shrine plinth and compact beacon.
- Broken ruin wall shapes.
- Command Hall and Barracks silhouettes from the existing fixture posture.
- Buildable ground and capture-site readability markers.
- Terrain-height suggestion through highland bands and terrace steps.
- Restrained teal Lume glow.

The blockout is intentionally visual enough for camera and composition review, but it is not final art.
