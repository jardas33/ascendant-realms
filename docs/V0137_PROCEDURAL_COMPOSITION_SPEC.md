# v0.137 Procedural Composition Spec

Classification: `BLOCKOUT_QUALITY_GREEN`

## Purpose

v0.137 upgrades the existing Godot player-facing Salto slice from functional primitive blockout toward an intentional top-down RTS/RPG battlefield composition while importing no art.

## Authored Procedural Battlefield Cues

- Salto foothold silhouette: layered ridge face, west/east notches, and highland shoulder pieces.
- wet-granite road: segmented road slabs with side paths to Barracks and ruins.
- Ford and water edge: stepping stones, shallow crossing strip, and cooler river-bank cues.
- Quarry and mine: deeper mine-mouth shadow, chisel face, tailings, lift arm, and ore slot.
- Shrine clearing: ringed clearing with restrained Lume braziers.
- Ruin pocket: broken arch, rubble, floor, and blocked edge.
- Barracks and Command Hall: readable footprints, split roof, hearth pool, and warm window accents.
- Buildable patches and blocked terrain: gridded friendly patches, fallen-pine cue, scree cue, and shelf edges.
- Tactical staging: friendly muster marks, Ashen approach lane, and severed Lume path segments.

## Boundaries

All changes are procedural Godot primitive meshes, materials, light, fog-like transparent bands, and text-scene posture. No generated images, imported assets, runtime art integration, browser runtime changes, save changes, stable-ID changes, full port, or final engine choice are included.
