# v0.10 Manual Tutorial v2 Playtest Checklist

Date: 2026-05-11

Audience: Emmanuel.

Use this checklist when you personally play Tutorial / Proving Grounds after the v0.10 copy and overlay polish.

## How To Start

1. Launch Ascendant Realms.
2. From the main menu, choose `Tutorial`.
3. Play through Tutorial / Proving Grounds at normal human speed.
4. Avoid using test shortcuts. Treat it like a first-time player would.

## During The Run

Watch for moments where you stop and wonder what to do next.

Pay special attention to:

- the first 30 seconds;
- selecting Aster;
- moving Aster and soldiers;
- capturing the Crown Shrine;
- understanding Crowns as battle-only resources;
- selecting the Command Hall;
- placing a Barracks;
- training a Militia;
- setting a rally point;
- casting Rally Banner;
- grouping up against Raider pressure;
- noticing that completion grants no rewards and saves nothing.

## Rate 1-5

Use 1 for poor/confusing and 5 for clear/good.

| Question | Rating |

## Questions To Answer

- Did any instruction confuse you?
- Did you feel lost at any point?
- Did the tutorial feel too long?
- Did it teach enough to start New Campaign?
- Did the no-reward completion feel acceptable?
- Was Exit Tutorial obvious enough?
- Did any overlay text feel too small or crowded?
- Did the enemy pressure feel fair after training a Militia?
- Would you skip this tutorial next time?

## What To Send Back

Please send:

- the ratings above;
- the step where confusion happened, if any;
- a screenshot if something looked crowded or unclear;
- what you expected the instruction to say;
- whether you would play it again or skip it next time;
- any moment that felt good and should be preserved.

## What Not To Request From This Checklist

Do not use this checklist to request:

- tutorial rewards;
- campaign progress;
- saved tutorial completion;
- new maps;
- new units;
- new factions;
- new workers or construction AI;
- generated art;
- runtime art replacement;
- broad UI redesign.

Those are separate larger goals and are intentionally out of scope for v0.10.
