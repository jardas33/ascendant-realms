## Quick 30-Minute Test

Goal: catch the biggest human-readability and pressure-noticeability issues quickly.

Use:

- Main packet.
- Baseline Cautious route card.
- Mission checklists for Tutorial / Proving Grounds, Border Village, one Cinderfen battle, Results screen, and Campaign return flow.
- Watchpoint rating sheet rows for pressure warning noticeability, objective tracker clarity, command feedback clarity, and results guidance.
- Summary form.

Suggested path:

1. Play Tutorial / Proving Grounds or at least launch it and complete early steps.
2. Start a clean campaign.
3. Play Border Village.
4. Play either Cinderfen Crossing or Cinderfen Watch from an available or prepared save.
5. Fill the summary form immediately.

Best output:

- One summary form.
- One or two confusion/pressure reports if anything felt bad.
- Ratings for clarity, fairness, pressure noticeability, and fun.

## Full 2-Hour Test

Goal: gather route-level evidence for the main v0.12.x balance watchpoints.

Use:

- Main packet.
- All relevant route cards.
- Mission checklists for Ashen Outpost, Cinderfen Crossing, Cinderfen Watch, Results screen, and Campaign return flow.
- Watchpoint rating sheet.
- Bug/friction templates as needed.
- Summary form.
- Designer interpretation guide after feedback is collected.

Suggested path:

1. Warm up with Tutorial / Proving Grounds or Border Village.
2. Play Baseline Cautious through Ashen Outpost and at least one Cinderfen battle.
3. Play one of No-Retinue, One-Veteran, or Mixed-Veterans if save setup allows.
4. Play Retinue + Training Yard II if a prepared save exists.
5. Play either Greedy Economy or Fast Army as a deliberate contrast route.
6. Fill watchpoint ratings and summary form.

Best output:

- One completed summary form.
- Watchpoint ratings for every tested route.
- Separate reports for any confusion, balance concern, pressure-warning miss, art/readability blocker, or actual bug.

## Stop Early If

- The build is not the current v0.12.x prototype.
- A save setup is unclear and would require editing data files.
- A bug prevents continuing.
- The session starts becoming a request for new content, new systems, art replacement, or balance implementation.
