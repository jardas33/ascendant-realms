# v0.137 Silhouette Refinement Spec

Classification: `BLOCKOUT_QUALITY_GREEN`

## Role Readability

The v0.137 blockout keeps future art-slot mapping unchanged and improves geometry differentiation rather than relying on color alone.

- Aster: tall hero body, command banner, blade, shield, crown disc, cloak profile, and restrained nameplate anchor.
- Worker: compact body, crate pack, tool handle/head, and low pick silhouette.
- Militia: shield, spear, spear tip, and square tabard.
- Ranger: narrow body, bow, quiver, arrow line, and hood peak.
- Ashen attacker: tapered hostile body, horns, forward blade, and leaning crest.
- Command Hall: keep tower, roof ridge, door slot, gabled shadow, banner, and warm hearth window.
- Barracks: split roof, training wings, weapon rack, drill-yard edge, footprint, and construction progress bar.
- Mine: quarry crane, mine-mouth shadow, lift arm, ore slot, and worked stone stack.
- Shrine: cap, beacon slot, steps, and paired Lume braziers.
- Ruin and Lume endpoint: broken arch/rubble plus glow core, readability ring, and future-art anchor.

## Acceptance Posture

The packaged slice must keep Aster, Worker, defenders, Ashen attackers, objectives, and structures readable at default zoom without extra debug labels or imported art.
