# v0.86 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

## Build

- Launch the v0.86 package.
- Start a normal campaign battle and a private Lume demo.
- Confirm no save prompt, migration message, or new settings step appears.

## Battlefield Shell

- Select a Command Hall, Barracks, Worker, capture site, and combat unit.
- Confirm action buttons show costs and short lock reasons immediately.
- Open command details and confirm longer copy is still available.

## Notifications

- Issue move, attack, build, train, and repair commands.
- Confirm routine messages are shorter and do not hide urgent warnings.
- Trigger an enemy pressure warning and confirm it reads as more urgent than routine chatter.

## Objective Tracker

- Confirm ordinary objectives still show progress.
- Confirm doctrine/event/Lume rows are compact and use details for longer counterplay.
- Confirm special-context battles do not show misleading `Objectives 0/0`.

## Battlefield Readability

- Inspect capture-site labels for neutral, owned, enemy, contested, and selected states when available.
- Select friendly and enemy units and confirm rings are clear without hiding combat.
- Inspect fog and minimap readability at 1920x1080 and 1366x768.

## Regression Checks

- Worker build, repair, and site assignment still work.
- Hero abilities and hotkeys still work.
- Control groups, Patrol, Retinue, Lume controls, and Results actions still work.
- Tutorial stays simple and no-save/no-reward.
