# v0.16.9 Tester Message Short

Date: 2026-05-22

Hi. This is a rough private prototype of Ascendant Realms, a small browser RTS/RPG.

Please use the included launcher or local server helper. Do not open `game/index.html` directly.

Your goal is to play your assigned route and report what confused you, what felt responsive, what felt unresponsive, and where you got stuck. Please do not focus on final art quality yet unless the visuals block understanding.

Please send back:

- assigned route
- browser and OS
- time played
- missions or modes played
- biggest confusion
- first control problem noticed
- whether attack, retreat, and selection felt clear
- whether Tutorial or Results explained what happened
- any bug that repeated
- screenshots or video only if useful

This is not a public build. Please do not repost the package or screenshots.
