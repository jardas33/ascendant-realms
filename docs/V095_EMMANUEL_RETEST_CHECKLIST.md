# v0.95 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

Estimated time: 8-10 minutes.

## Launch

1. Open the latest v0.95 playtest package.
2. Start a normal campaign battle from the campaign map.

## Battle Readability

1. Look at the battlefield for five seconds before clicking anything.
2. Confirm whether roads, water, buildable ground, blocked areas, and capture sites are easier to distinguish.
3. Select the hero, Worker, melee unit, ranged unit, Command Hall, and a capture site.
4. Confirm whether each selected object reads clearly without final art.
5. Capture a neutral site, then inspect friendly, enemy, selected, objective, and contested site states if possible.
6. Check the minimap while the camera is near the base and near a capture site.
7. Launch the private Lume demo and toggle Links Auto, Hidden, and Always.

## Questions

- Does the battlefield feel less like debug geometry?
- Are fog and terrain readable enough for placeholder art?
- Are unit silhouettes distinct enough to identify roles?
- Are capture-site rings and labels helpful or too loud?
- Are routine labels quieter without making the battle confusing?
- Is the minimap easier to read?
- What is still ugly, confusing, or too noisy?
