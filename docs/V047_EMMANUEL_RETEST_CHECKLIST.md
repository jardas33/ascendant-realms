# v0.47 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

Date: 2026-05-29

Use a clean package named `ascendant-realms-private-playtest-<commit>` for this retest. Do not use a package with a `-dirty` suffix for outside testing.

## Fresh Act 1 Start

- Start a new campaign after optionally practicing Tutorial / Proving Grounds.
- Confirm Border Village is available and Old Stone Road is locked.
- Select Border Village and confirm it says Act 1 Step 2: First Campaign Battle.
- Confirm the node copy says it is the first persistent campaign battle, not the no-reward Tutorial.

## First Clear and Replay

- Complete Border Village.
- Confirm Results show Old Stone Road as the next unlocked mission.
- Return to Campaign Map.
- Confirm Border Village is replayable and Old Stone Road is available.
- Select completed Border Village and confirm replay copy says first-clear rewards do not duplicate.

## Base Development and Resource Control

- Select Old Stone Road.
- Confirm it says Act 1 Step 3: Base Development and includes Worker/building/production reminders.
- Complete Old Stone Road.
- Confirm Aether Well Ruins, Bandit Hillfort, Refugee Caravan, and Marcher Camp become available.
- Select Aether Well Ruins and confirm resource-site assignment / upgrade guidance is visible.

## Champion Relic Milestone

- Reach or seed Ashen Outpost.
- Confirm it says Act 1 Step 6: Champion Relic Milestone and previews Captain Malrec, Fortified Enemy, relic eligibility, and preparation copy.
- Defeat Captain Malrec and complete the mission.
- Confirm Results show relic choice, skill-point or skill-tree reminder when relevant, equip relic guidance, and replay optional objective copy.

## Tutorial Protection

- Launch Tutorial / Proving Grounds.
- Confirm it remains no-save and no-reward.
- Confirm no relic reward choice, campaign node reward, replay reward, or Act 1 campaign completion state appears from Tutorial.

## What Not To Judge Yet

- New map variety, final campaign cinematics, full quest journal, mission medals, shop/crafting, new art, or broad AI/pathing behavior.
