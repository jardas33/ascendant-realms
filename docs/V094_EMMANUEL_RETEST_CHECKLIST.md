# v0.94 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

## Goal

Review the out-of-battle presentation rescue in under ten minutes.

## Package

Use the final v0.94 private playtest package generated after verification.

## Main Menu

- Does the menu feel intentionally composed on desktop?
- Is New Campaign clearly the primary action?
- Are Practice and Manage actions easy to understand?

## Ascendant Creation

- Can you compare classes within a few seconds?
- Is the selected class obvious?
- Is the selected origin obvious?
- Is Begin Campaign obvious?
- Are the More Details disclosures enough for longer prose?

## Campaign Shell

- Is the map immediately readable?
- Is Salto Outskirts selected on a fresh campaign?
- Are future locked routes quieter than available routes?
- Is the selected route clear?
- Does the mission panel feel compact enough?

## Campaign Tabs

- Check Stronghold, Hero, Inventory, Intel, and Reputation.
- Is each tab easier to scan?
- Are optional details less noisy?
- Is any useful action missing?

## Results

- Finish or open a normal Results screen.
- Is compact Results still concise?
- Expand Show Full Battle Details.
- Are the grouped details easier to scan?

## Report Back

- What still feels confusing?
- What still feels ugly?
- What action was hardest to find?
