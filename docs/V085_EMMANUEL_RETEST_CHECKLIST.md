# v0.85 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

Use the clean v0.85 private playtest package.

## Lume Overlay

1. Launch the private Aether Well Lume Demo.
2. Confirm the Lume tracker shows `Links: Auto`.
3. Confirm inactive links do not fill the battlefield.
4. Capture West Stone Cut and Ford Toll.
5. Confirm the first link pulses bright, then fades to a subtle stable line.
6. Select West Stone Cut or Ford Toll and confirm the line highlights while selected.
7. Switch to `Links: Always` and confirm eligible links remain visible.
8. Switch to `Links: Hidden` and confirm stable lines disappear while major transition pulses still show.
9. Confirm North Aether Spring is not promoted before the first link awakens.

## Private Demo Results

1. Click `Finish Demo & View Results`.
2. Confirm the heading is `PRIVATE DEMO COMPLETE`, not `Victory`.
3. Confirm the subtitle says rewards and campaign progress were not saved.
4. Confirm the Lume Network summary is understandable without scrolling.
5. Confirm `Return to Campaign Map`, `Replay Lume Demo`, and `Show Full Battle Details` are above the fold.
6. Expand `Show Full Battle Details` and confirm the normal telemetry is still available.
7. Return to Campaign Map and confirm campaign progress, rewards, hero XP, Retinue, relics, and reputation did not change.

## Safety Checks

1. Complete or inspect a normal mission Results screen and confirm it still uses ordinary `Victory` posture.
2. Launch Tutorial and confirm no Lume overlay controls appear.
3. Launch an unrelated mission and confirm no Lume overlay controls appear.
