## Captain Malrec Progression

- Early Act 1 keeps Malrec off-screen while Salto learns survival, economy, and site control.
- Mid Act 1 frames the rival as disciplined pressure around roads, shrines, and Lume risk.
- Ashen Outpost makes Malrec the clear finale focus without changing commander AI, rewards, or phase rules.
- v0.99 copy presents Malrec as dangerous and partially understandable rather than a generic raider.

## Audit Outcome

The Act 1 route now reads as a journey from Salto outward: foothold, road, camp, Lume pressure, chapel support, hillfort pressure, and Ashen Outpost finale. Long explanations remain available behind existing disclosures; the default read is shorter and mission-led.
