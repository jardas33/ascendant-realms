# v0.49 Act 1 Balance And Telemetry Report

## Balance Position

v0.49 applies no gameplay number changes. The deterministic telemetry does not show a structural unfair-opening problem:

- Border Village and Old Stone Road are stable across all scripted routes.
- Safe Beginner clears Aether Well Ruins, Bandit Hillfort, and Ashen Outpost.
- Failures on the harder Act 1 nodes are concentrated in greedy, rushed, or clear-speed scripts.
- First combat timing remains readable enough for a Worker/building opening in the deterministic model.

## Conservative Polish Applied

Copy and guidance now more directly say:

- Train Workers early and keep production active on Old Stone Road.
- Hold two sites when possible, assign Workers, upgrade safe income, and rebuild before pushing Aether Well Ruins.
- Do not rush Bandit Hillfort; build a mixed army, hold after pressure, and attack after stabilizing.
- Spend skill points, equip relics/gear, stage troops, and push Ashen Outpost after the fortress wave.
- Replay copy should mention already-claimed rewards, reduced replay rewards, and open optional objectives.

## Farming Safety

No reward model changed. Existing rules remain:

- Tutorial / Proving Grounds grants no persistent save or reward state.
- First-clear campaign rewards are one-time.
- Replay rewards stay reduced.
- Unique relics cannot be repeatedly farmed.
- Optional objective completion credit does not duplicate.

## Manual Balance Watchpoints

1. Aether Well Ruins should feel like resource-control pressure, not a center-rush trap.
2. Bandit Hillfort should teach staging and commander discipline, not surprise-spike the player.
3. Ashen Outpost should feel harder but fair, with relic/skill reminders landing before replay or Chapter 2.

## Deferred

- No numeric rebalance until human/manual evidence reproduces a specific unfairness pattern.
- No broad AI or pathing changes.
- No new Act 1 mission content.
