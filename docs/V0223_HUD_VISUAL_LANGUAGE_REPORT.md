# v0.223 HUD Visual Language Report

Date: 2026-06-17

## Result

PASS. The opt-in presentation reboot now uses a compact Barrosan HUD language with charcoal panel bodies, restrained timber/granite/iron trim, muted teal aether accents, warm focus accents and ember hostile accents.

All icons are original procedural SVG strings rasterized at their intended display size. No image was generated or downloaded and no runtime art slot was added.

## Visual Review

- The battlefield remains dominant at all required resolutions.
- Default fixed UI occupancy remains `0.0805`; expanded occupancy remains at or below `0.2227`.
- Resource, command, production and utility icons remain contained at small sizes.
- Tooltips are docked to the right edge and remain outside the battlefield center.
- Build and train drawers remain contextual rather than persistent.
- The contact sheet is `artifacts/manual-review/v0223-hud-visual-language/12_contact_sheet.png`.

The screenshots are materially more deliberate than the v0.222 baseline, though the environment remains intentionally muted and still trails the supplied reference in landmark density, structure detail and overall color separation.
