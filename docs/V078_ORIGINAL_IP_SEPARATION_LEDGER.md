# v0.78 Original IP Separation Ledger

Date: 2026-05-30

## Status

This is a product and creative risk ledger, not legal advice. Commercial launch requires proper human legal and trademark review.

## Allowed Inspiration Categories

The game may be inspired by broad RTS/RPG pleasures:

- persistent heroes,
- Race + Class combinations,
- armies,
- Workers,
- mines,
- site conversion,
- resource production,
- faction-specific units,
- faction-specific buildings,
- campaign progression,
- skirmish,
- future multiplayer.

These are broad genre concepts. They must be implemented with original names, art, lore, code, UI, maps, audio, and campaign expression.

## Prohibited Copying

Do not copy:

- names,
- faction names,
- race rosters,
- characters,
- lore,
- dialogue,
- maps,
- campaign scripts,
- unit rosters,
- unit designs,
- buildings,
- icons,
- skill trees,
- UI layouts,
- music,
- sound effects,
- art,
- code,
- animations,
- logos,
- one-to-one recreation of another game's expression.

## Title And Trademark Risk

`JARDAS`, `Oath of the Barrosan Marches`, `The Lume Wars`, `Crown of Barroso`, and related terms are not legally cleared. Review must check trademark conflicts, store searchability, domains, localization, and similarity to existing games/books/films.

## AI-Assisted Art Risk

AI-assisted ideation can accidentally resemble protected designs. Mitigate with prompt controls, style bibles, silhouette sheets, originality review, source/license metadata, and no runtime inclusion without approval.

## Human Legal Review Required

Before commercial announcement or sale, human legal review should examine:

- title/trademark clearance,
- faction/race names,
- character names,
- logo concepts,
- visual similarities,
- music/audio sources,
- purchased or generated asset licenses,
- open-source dependency compliance,
- storefront screenshots and marketing copy.

## v0.78 Boundary

This ledger does not approve runtime naming, art, asset, title, or marketing use. It prepares decisions for Emmanuel review.
