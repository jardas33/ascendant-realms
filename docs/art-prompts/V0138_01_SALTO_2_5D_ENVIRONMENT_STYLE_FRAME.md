## Copy-Ready Prompt

Create one reference-only environment style frame for an original fantasy RTS/RPG called JARDAS: Oath of the Barrosan Marches. Show the Salto highland foothold as a super-cool 2026 fixed-camera 2.5D RTS/RPG battlefield, original Barrosan highland fantasy, not a clone of any existing game. The view is high three-quarter orthographic with clear tactical readability from RTS command distance. Compose wet granite roads, a shallow ford, quarry-cut mine terraces, a small Lume shrine clearing, ruined stone pockets, buildable earth near a modest Barracks footprint, and an Ashen approach lane. Use atmospheric terrain, modern restrained lighting, crisp silhouettes, readable path/blocker/water/resource zones, and subtle teal Lume energy. Make the terrain polished and evocative but not muddy, not overdark, not cluttered, and not generic mobile-game fantasy. Leave UI-safe margins and keep landmarks instantly identifiable.

Original IP only. Evoke the spirit of a modern top-down RTS/RPG with a persistent hero and tactical readability, while avoiding direct Warlords Battlecry imitation or any protected game, faction, logo, UI, building, map, creature, character, symbol, or art-style lookalike.
