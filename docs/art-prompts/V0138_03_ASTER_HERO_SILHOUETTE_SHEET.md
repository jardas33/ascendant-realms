## Copy-Ready Prompt

Create one reference-only silhouette exploration sheet for Aster, the central persistent hero of an original fantasy RTS/RPG called JARDAS: Oath of the Barrosan Marches. Show 6 to 8 clear fixed-camera gameplay silhouettes for Aster at RTS/RPG command distance, with a Barrosan highland identity: travel-worn cloak, practical light armor, command staff or banner-spear, readable shoulder profile, subtle Lume ward accent, and a heroic but grounded stance. Include front, three-quarter, side, and small top-down readability views on a plain neutral background. Focus on silhouette clarity, faction identity, selection readability, and a shape that can be recognized when tiny. Keep the design original, highland-fantasy, and tactical rather than portrait-led.

Original IP only. Evoke the spirit of a modern 2026 fixed-camera fantasy RTS/RPG hero without copying Warlords Battlecry, Warcraft, Diablo, Dragon Age, Lord of the Rings, or any protected character, faction, armor silhouette, weapon, symbol, pose, or art style.
