Show 6 to 8 clear fixed-camera gameplay silhouettes at RTS/RPG command distance, with practical highland labor identity: short work cloak, broad tool belt, quarry hammer or pick, small pack frame, rolled sleeves or bracers, sturdy boots, and simple Lume-safe work lantern.
