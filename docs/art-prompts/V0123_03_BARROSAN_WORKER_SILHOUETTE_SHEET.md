Status: copy-ready reference prompt only. Do not import generated images into runtime.

## Prompt

Create a reference-only silhouette sheet for the Barrosan Worker in an original fixed-camera fantasy RTS/RPG called Ascendant Realms. Show 10 readable worker silhouettes at RTS scale and three larger detail sketches. The Worker should look like a practical highland builder and site hand: tools, bundled supplies, timber frame gear, quarry-ready posture, and no heroic armor. Camera and scale must prioritize top-down tactical readability. Lighting should be clean studio lighting with clear value separation. Materials should suggest worn cloth, leather straps, timber, iron tools, and wet highland grit.

Original IP only. The Worker should support a super-cool 2026 RTS/RPG visual ambition while remaining humble, readable, and distinct from famous RTS peasant, villager, acolyte, or builder units.

Required anchors: camera RTS-readable three-quarter and top-down-friendly poses; scale small worker gameplay read plus larger detail sketches; lighting clean studio value separation; material worn cloth, leather straps, timber, iron tools, and wet highland grit; silhouette practical builder/support profile with tools and carried materials.

Negative guidance: no protected lookalikes, no copied RTS worker silhouettes, no Warcraft peon/peasant look, no Age of Empires villager clone, no mobile-game mascot proportions, no oversized fantasy hammer unless still practical, no final sprite sheet, no runtime-ready asset.

This is reference-only. Runtime integration is explicitly forbidden.

## Human Review Checklist

- Worker reads as a builder/support unit at small scale.
- Tools and carried materials are clear but not cluttered.
- Silhouette differs from hero and military units.
- Barrosan highland identity is visible.
- No protected-IP lookalike risk.
