## Approved Meaning Of Jardas

A Jardas is an oath-bound hero capable of awakening, binding, defending, and guiding Lume sites without treating the living land as a consequence-free commodity.

The protagonist is a newly awakened Jardas.

This meaning is now a central identity pillar for future lore and gameplay planning. It is not runtime lore copy yet.
