# v0.84 Emmanuel Retest Checklist

Use the clean v0.84 private playtest package.

## Private Demo Launch

1. Start a new campaign or open a locked Aether Well route.
2. Use the private Aether Well Lume Demo launcher.
3. Confirm the battle shows `PRIVATE DEMO - rewards and campaign progress disabled`.
4. Confirm `Exit Demo` is visible immediately.

## Guided Lume Loop

1. Confirm the tracker starts with `LUME WARD`, `Capture West Stone Cut`, and `LUME LINKS 0/2`.
2. Capture West Stone Cut.
3. Confirm the tracker asks for Ford Toll and says `1 of 2 sites held`.
4. Capture Ford Toll.
5. Confirm `LUME WARD ACTIVE`, `LUME LINKS 1/2`, and `Nearby allies take 8% less damage`.
6. Confirm `Focus North Aether Spring` appears.
7. Confirm the battlefield shows a visible teal link and endpoint halos.

## Fast Retest Closeout

1. Click `Finish Demo & View Results`.
2. Confirm Results says the private demo is no-save/no-reward.
3. Confirm Results includes Lume telemetry.
4. Return to Campaign Map.
5. Confirm campaign progress, rewards, hero XP, Retinue, relics, and reputation did not change.

## Exit Path

1. Relaunch the private demo.
2. Click `Exit Demo`.
3. Confirm the Campaign Map returns without Results and without battle persistence.
