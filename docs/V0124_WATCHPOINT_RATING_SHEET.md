## Evidence Hints

Good evidence:

- "Cinderfen Watch, Fast Army, won with 7 units left, noticed pressure warning while fighting at road, had time to retreat."
- "Greedy Economy, Crossing, floated 3000+ Crowns but kept delaying Barracks production; timeout felt like my mistake."
- "Retinue + Training Yard II, Ashen, ignored shrine and still won with no losses twice."

Weak evidence:

- "Too hard" with no route, node, army state, or result.
- "Too easy" after one run with an unknown save.
- "Looks bad" when the note does not say whether gameplay understanding was blocked.

## Action Guidance

Choose `no change` when the route was readable, fair, and fun enough.

Choose `copy/readability` when you understood the mechanics after the fact but the game text or UI did not guide you soon enough.

Choose `tiny tuning` only when repeated runs show the same unfairness or trivialization with clear evidence.

Choose `needs more testing` when the issue is plausible but not repeated.

Choose `future art/UI overhaul` when the problem is mostly visual quality, landmark art, minimap icon language, or layout density.

Choose `future systems pass` when the fix would require new mechanics, enemy construction, live reinforcements, reward redesign, or broad campaign/economy changes.
