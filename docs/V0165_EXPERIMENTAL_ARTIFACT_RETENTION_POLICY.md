# v0.165 Experimental Artifact Retention Policy

Status: `ACTIVE_DRY_RUN_POLICY`

## Retain

Retain:

- selected local Worker source and metadata
- selected local Barracks material source and metadata
- selected local Militia source and metadata
- tracked diagnostic fallbacks
- tracked source, tooling, and docs
- current v0.165 validation, capture, benchmark, audit, Computer Use, and boundary evidence
- current human-review scorecard

## Archive Candidates

Older comparator screenshots, older benchmark captures, older generated reports, and superseded local derivatives may be archive candidates. They are not deleted in v0.165.

## Deletion Candidates

Only clearly disposable, regenerable temporary validation/cache residue may be future delete candidates. v0.165 leaves them intact unless separately proven and approved.

## Manual Approval

Unknown files require Emmanuel approval before deletion or movement. The cleanup tool is dry-run by default. Any deletion must be a separately approved checkpoint, not v0.165.
