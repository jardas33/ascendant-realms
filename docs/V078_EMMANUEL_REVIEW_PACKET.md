# v0.78 Emmanuel Review Packet

Date: 2026-05-30

## One-Page Summary

v0.78 proposes a stronger original identity for the current browser RTS/RPG prototype without changing runtime behavior. `Ascendant Realms` remains the internal codename. The leading public title proposal is `JARDAS: Oath of the Barrosan Marches`, with `JARDAS` as the dominant logo word.

The world direction is a dark heroic-fantasy frontier inspired by Barroso and northern highlands without literally reproducing Portugal. The opening home is `Salto`; the region is `The Barrosan Marches`; the living land-power is `Lume`; the protagonist is a newly awakened `Jardas`, an oath-bound hero who can awaken and defend Lume sites but must not exploit them without consequence.

Captain Malrec remains the Act 1 rival, reframed as intelligent, charismatic, dangerous, and partially understandable: he believes controlling Lume is necessary to prevent destruction.

## Eight-Race Summary

| Race | Species | Battlefield identity | Lume relationship |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Barrosan Freeholds | Human | resilient defense, Workers, Retinue, site control | stewardship |
| Ashen Covenant | mixed altered civilization | pressure, burn, siege, overcharge | controlled overuse |
| Moura Court | subterranean fae | deception, hidden routes, elites | bargains and springs |
| Granitborn | living stone | durability, living walls, territory | stone memory |
| Wolfveil Clans | lupine beastkin | speed, scouting, flanking | pack territory |
| Careto Host | winter spirits/possessed warbands | disruption, tempo, fear | ritual inversion |
| Rootbound Concord | plant/fungal beings | regeneration, ecosystems, terrain | growth and healing |
| Deepbell League | subterranean smithfolk/helpers | mines, tunnels, traps, machinery | engineered resonance |

The roster is intentionally one human faction, one mixed faction, and six strongly non-human factions. The silhouette test is strongest for Ashen, Granitborn, Wolfveil, Careto, Rootbound, and Deepbell if art direction enforces non-human anatomy and architecture.

## Hero Architecture

Future heroes should combine Race + Class + Origin + Oath specialization, then grow through skill points, equipment, relics, and unlimited levelling with soft scaling. Proposed class directions: Marshal, Warden, Seer, Binder, Hunter, Artificer, Shepherd.

Existing Warlord can map toward Marshal/Warden, Arcanist toward Seer/Binder, and Shepherd can remain Shepherd. No migration is approved now.

## Visual Direction

Target: modern dark heroic-fantasy RTS/RPG with grounded Barrosan highland inspiration, readable tactical battlefields, strong silhouettes, tactile UI, fog, rain, wet terrain, granite, hearth fire, shrines, springs, mines, old roads, winter masks, and distinctive non-human races.

Principle: tactical readability first.

No art was generated or imported.

## Browser-To-Desktop Direction

The current browser game remains the correct development prototype. The long-term target is a real installable desktop game, but not a browser game merely wrapped inside an executable.

Stages:

1. Browser Prototype: prove the loop and stabilize QA.
2. Desktop Vertical Slice: later representative PC build and engine/packaging decision.
3. Full Desktop Game: long campaign, races, skirmish, replayability, future PvP/co-op.

No desktop port or engine choice is approved now.

## Original-IP Separation

Broad genre inspiration is allowed. Protected expression is not. Do not copy names, faction rosters, characters, lore, dialogue, maps, unit designs, buildings, icons, UI layouts, music, art, code, animations, logos, or one-to-one expression from another game.

Human legal/trademark review remains required.
