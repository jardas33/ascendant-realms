## Limits

- The lab cannot measure human attention, stress, fun, or visual readability.
- Hero final HP/death state and base damage are unavailable in current telemetry.
- Profile rows can overlap by design; the lab compares evidence views, not independent player populations.
- Treat automated results as prioritization evidence for human testing, not as proof that tuning is required.
